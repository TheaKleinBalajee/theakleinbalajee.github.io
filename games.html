Path of Emotions

Part of my internship with ResolveX. A pixel art typing game designed for crisis line workers to build fluency with emotional vocabulary and improve their typing skills. Players type feeling words to help a character climb a mountain. Naming feelings is an important step toward helping others navigate them.
